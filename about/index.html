About

Established in 2006, Graphic Graffiti Designs is the 'shopfront' agency for freelance photographer and web designer Tim Howard. Graphic Graffiti Designs specialises in coding 'beautiful' websites, using valid code adhering to strict W3C standards. Our expertise extends across HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL and Ruby on Rails on the web development side, to portrait and product photography on the photographic side, to social advertising and search engine optimisation, as a companion product to our web development services.
